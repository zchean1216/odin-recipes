Mac and Cheese

Description

Instant noodles are a quick and easy meal that can be enjoyed any time of day. With just a few simple ingredients, you can elevate your instant noodles to a delicious and satisfying dish. This recipe provides a basic way to prepare instant noodles, but feel free to add your favorite vegetables, proteins, and toppings to make it your own.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack instant noodles
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • 1 green onion, chopped (optional)
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken, beef, or tofu (optional)
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring 2 cups of water to a boil in a pot.
  2. Add the instant noodles and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until the noodles are tender.
  3. If adding an egg, crack it into the pot and gently stir to create an egg drop soup effect, or let it poach for a whole egg.
  4. Add the seasoning packet that comes with the instant noodles, stirring well to combine.
  5. If adding cooked chicken, beef, tofu, or vegetables, stir them into the pot and heat through.
  6. Once everything is heated and well combined, pour the noodles and broth into a bowl.
  7. Top with chopped green onion if desired.
  8. Enjoy your delicious and upgraded instant noodles!
